What is SAMADHAN?
Objective:The objective of this nationwide hackathon is to gather the young and innovative minds to establish a community where students from all over the country come up with smart solutions, mostly related to digitalization, and help to improve the functioning of society as a whole. There are several administrative apps as well as industrial and social innovations that help to improve the quality of life of the people.
Problem statement: Open for all real-world problems that can be solved using Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain/Web3, Cyber Security, and Open Innovation.

How to register.
  • Team leader must visit the official SAMADHAN 1.0 website Or Register directly through the LINK HERE
  • After the team leader's registration, he or she will get a team code.
  • This team code will be shared with other team members during their registration.
  • Members who use the same code will only be considered members of the same team.
  • Each team member must register individually.
  • Individual participation is not permitted.
  • During registration, there is no requirement for a project title.
  • There can be 3 minimum members in a team and 5 maximum.
